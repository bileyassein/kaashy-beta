Installation Guide

  1. Tap the Download APK button above.
  2. Submit your Gmail address to us (you'll receive a Firebase invite).
  3. Go to your email inbox and open the invitation email from Firebase App Distribution.
  4. Tap the invitation link and follow the steps to download and install the app securely.
  5. If installation is blocked, enable "Install from unknown sources" in your device settings.
  6. Once the app is installed, open it and register using your phone number.
  7. If your browser prompts you, allow the download.
